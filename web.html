Beauty

"Water does not resist. Water flows.
When you plunge your hand into it, all you feel is a caress.”

The water understands
Civilization well;
It wets my foot, but prettily,
It chills my life, but wittily,
It is not disconcerted,
It is not broken-hearted:
Well used, it decketh joy,
Adorneth, doubleth joy:

Construction

“Construction is the art of making a

The Crane Operator

He knows which lever to pull
and how to lift that tree,
he's checked the size and weight;
those details are the key
to doing his job, and doing it right—
he raises and lowers
the boom day and night;
he figures the distance, he studies the chart—
operating a crane is construction art.
